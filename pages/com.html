  • Commercial / Home-use Elevator

    There is a great demand for compact home elevators in individual bungalows and residential requirements and to cater to this segment, we offer different types and sizes of home elevators. We offer the regular traction elevator with a capacity of 4 or 6 passengers to cater to typical small family needs. This system will require regular machine room arrangement for installation and three phase power supply for its operation. We also have Single Phase Gearless MRL Villa elevators for passenger capacity up to 5 passengers. We also have in our range Hydraulic home lift with a passenger capacity of 3 persons and this system does not require a special machine room and the power pack can be accommodated anywhere in the ground floor of the building. This also has an advantage that it operates with a normal power supply of single phase.
    Villa lift has been mainly designed according to the requirements from extensive family users.
    It features as small covering space, none lift wall, little noise, convenient installation, fine safety and reliability.
    It is an ideal household elevator for the duplex and multi-storey families.
    It is also the most ideal up & down means of transportation for the old, the sick and the disabled.
    Environmental protection and energy-saving: Regarding single phase 220V voltage, the power loss is equal to that of one refrigerator.
